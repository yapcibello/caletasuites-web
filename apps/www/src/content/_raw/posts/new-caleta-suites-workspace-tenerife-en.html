At Caleta Suites, we continue to evolve to offer our guests experiences that blend wellbeing, inspiration and functionality. Starting in January 2026, we’ll be opening the new Caleta Suites Workspace — a space designed for those who seek a place to rest, create and focus, surrounded by the unique atmosphere of La Caleta Costa Adeje.

The Caleta Suites Workspace was created in response to a new lifestyle — one where people can work from anywhere in the world, and choose Tenerife for its climate, peace and quality of life. Here, work and leisure coexist naturally: spend your mornings on your projects and your afternoons by the sea, on the golf course or discovering the local gastronomy.

Inspiration, calm and design at the new Caleta Suites Workspace

The new Caleta Suites Workspace has been conceived as a bright, modern and welcoming environment, where every detail encourages focus and creativity.

Its interiors reflect the contemporary, elegant style that defines Caleta Suites — natural wood, clean lines, warm light and shared areas designed to connect and inspire in a relaxed setting.

It will offer spacious and serene areas, with both individual and shared workstations, quiet zones and an atmosphere ideal for remote work, writing, design or simply letting ideas flow.

A new way to understand remote work: stress-free, noise-free, and powered by the energy of the Atlantic Ocean.

Live, work and enjoy in La Caleta Costa Adeje

Staying at Caleta Suites means experiencing La Caleta at its best — its sea, gastronomy, walks and peaceful rhythm. And now, with the new Caleta Suites Workspace, you can work remotely without giving up the southern Tenerife lifestyle.

Imagine starting your day in a calm, sunlit space and, when the work is done, heading out for a round at Golf Costa Adeje, a seaside walk, or dinner at one of the area’s renowned restaurants. Here, every day naturally combines productivity and wellbeing.

If you’re looking for the perfect balance between work and relaxation, Caleta Suites will soon be your new favourite place.
