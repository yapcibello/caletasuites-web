La Caleta, with its calm sea and mild climate even in the final days of December, offers the ideal setting to celebrate the end of the year in a different way. For guests staying at Caleta Suites, the plan becomes even more appealing: just a short walk away, Restaurante La Vieja hosts a gala dinner by the Atlantic—perfect for those looking for an elegant New Year’s Eve with a taste of the sea.

Located on the seafront promenade and considered a gastronomic landmark in southern Tenerife, La Vieja has built its identity around local, high-quality products. Its cuisine is guided by a simple and honest philosophy: the sea is at the heart of everything. Freshly sourced fish and seafood arrive daily, shaping a menu where origin and respect for the product come first.

This year, the restaurant celebrates New Year’s Eve with an elegant, cinema-inspired proposal. The gala embraces a Red Carpet aesthetic—a nod to Hollywood’s magic—combined with the charm of the ocean just a few metres away. Live music and a carefully designed atmosphere blend the restaurant’s marine character with the elegance of the occasion, creating the perfect setting to bid farewell to 2025 by the sea.

A gala menu with the sea as the star

The culinary proposal remains true to the restaurant’s philosophy, placing seafood at the centre of the experience. The evening begins with a selection of appetisers: a mini marinated tuna tartare with quail egg yolk, a brioche with dill-marinated salmon and cream cheese, and a carabinero croquette that captures the essence of La Vieja.

The starter features a carpaccio of Mediterranean red prawn finished with Ocetra caviar. This is followed by a first course of Cantabrian lobster cannelloni with a rich seafood suquet, and a main dish of monkfish in green sauce with clams. To end the night on a sweet note, the menu includes a mille-feuille filled with nougat cream—a festive, creamy finale.

The best plan for guests at Caleta Suites

The short distance between Caleta Suites and La Vieja allows guests to enjoy the gala dinner without transfers or rush. The combination of comfort, a privileged setting and a cuisine defined by the finest ingredients makes this one of the most appealing options to celebrate New Year’s Eve in La Caleta.
