Romantic dining near Caleta Suites

One of the highlights of Valentine’s Day in La Caleta is the culinary scene. Just steps from Caleta Suites, La Vieja stands out as a classic choice for a romantic lunch or dinner by the sea. Known for its fresh fish and seafood, this restaurant offers an authentic dining experience rooted in local tradition, paired with ocean views that elevate any special occasion.

For couples seeking a more elevated gastronomic experience, Costa Adeje is home to several Michelin-starred restaurants within easy reach. El Rincón de Juan Carlos, for example, represents one of the highest expressions of contemporary Spanish cuisine in Tenerife, offering an intimate and carefully crafted dining experience that is ideal for a special celebration.

To start the day slowly, Matisse Coffee & Brunch, located within the same complex as Caleta Suites, is perfect for a relaxed breakfast or brunch. From pancakes and eggs to fresh juices and specialty coffee, it is an ideal spot to enjoy a quiet morning together without leaving La Caleta.

Wellness and relaxation for two

Valentine’s Day is also an opportunity to focus on wellbeing. The south of Tenerife offers several spa experiences designed to be enjoyed as a couple. Just a short drive away, Sándara Wellness & Spa at Abama Resort Tenerife combines water circuits, treatments and relaxation areas in a serene setting. Similarly, Bahía Wellness Retreat at Bahía del Duque provides holistic experiences that blend spa rituals with gastronomy, creating a balanced and indulgent escape.

These spa experiences fit perfectly into a Valentine’s Day stay at Caleta Suites, adding an extra layer of relaxation to your time together.

Nature, calm and shared moments

Beyond dining and wellness, La Caleta invites couples to enjoy simple pleasures: coastal walks at sunset, quiet beaches, and moments of calm by the ocean. February’s mild temperatures make the south of Tenerife especially appealing for outdoor plans, whether it’s a seaside stroll or simply enjoying the views from your terrace.
