La Caleta, a place renowned for its crystal-clear waters and stunning landscapes, is an ideal destination for sports lovers. This coastal area offers a wide range of outdoor activities to enjoy both alone and in groups. From water-based adventures to relaxing walks, here are four sports you can enjoy in La Caleta.

For those who enjoy action, it’s time to grab a board and head into the ocean. The beaches of La Caleta, and Costa Adeje in general, are ideal for surfing and bodyboarding. In the area or just a short distance away, there are several surf schools and clubs where you can rent all the necessary equipment or even book lessons, whether you want to improve your technique or try these sports for the first time.

If you are looking for something a little less conventional but just as exciting, kayaking is an excellent option. This activity allows you to experience the coastline from a completely different perspective.

One of the main advantages of kayaking is that it is a low-impact activity, making it suitable for all fitness levels and ages. In La Caleta, you will find companies offering different types of kayak tours, some of which come with an added bonus: La Caleta is home to many sea turtles. You may even have the chance to see them up close and swim alongside them.

Beyond turtles, there is plenty more marine life to discover. La Caleta is a natural reserve where a wide variety of underwater species coexist. Did you know you can explore them yourself? Activities such as snorkelling allow you to enjoy a kind of “underwater walk” while discovering Tenerife’s rich marine environment.

Colourful fish, corals, manta rays, stingrays… what are you waiting for to book this experience?

Finally, for those who prefer activities on land, La Caleta also offers several trekking options, suitable for experienced hikers, beginners and families alike. Each route is different and reveals unforgettable views of the natural beauty that defines this coastal enclave.
