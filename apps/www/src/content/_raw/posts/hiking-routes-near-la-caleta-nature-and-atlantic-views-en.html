La Caleta is one of the most special spots in southern Tenerife for anyone who enjoys the outdoors. Its coastal setting, mild climate and seaside paths make it an ideal starting point to explore nature without travelling far from your accommodation. Staying at Caleta Suites gives you easy access to scenic, accessible routes that combine activity, wellbeing and spectacular views throughout your stay.

Coastal walks from La Caleta

One of the most popular routes is the coastal walk that connects La Caleta with Puerto Colón. It’s an easy, comfortable path with uninterrupted views of the Atlantic. Covering around eight kilometres round-trip, it’s perfect for an early-morning stroll, a sunset walk or simply stretching your legs after a training session or an active day on the island.

Another local favourite is the route from La Caleta to El Puertito de Armeñime. This coastal track takes you through volcanic landscapes, natural coves and open sea views, passing by unique spots such as Los Morteros and the well-known Diego Hernández beach. It’s a moderate route, ideal for those looking for a more natural and unspoilt environment without moving far from the village.

Protected nature on the coastline

The protected natural area surrounding the lower part of La Caleta offers dirt paths with panoramic views, cliffs and native flora. It’s the perfect setting to switch off, walk at your own pace and enjoy the landscape in a quiet, open environment. Whether you’re looking for a short stroll or a longer walk, this area invites you to breathe fresh air and appreciate the coastline from a different perspective.
