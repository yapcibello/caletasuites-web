Beach clubs in South Tenerife have become one of the most sought-after plans for those looking to enjoy the ocean through a complete lifestyle experience combining gastronomy, music and atmosphere. In areas such as Costa Adeje and Playa de las Américas, these venues offer a premium daytime leisure concept by the sea.

From La Caleta, it is easy to access some of the most attractive beach clubs in the south of the island.

Monkey Beach Club: a complete seaside experience

Among beach clubs in South Tenerife, Monkey Beach Club stands out as one of the most iconic and complete venues in Costa Adeje. Located right on the seafront, it brings together different gastronomic and lifestyle concepts in one place.

Its offer is divided into several spaces. Playa Club focuses on relaxed seaside lunches with fresh Mediterranean cuisine. Bésame Mucho represents its more refined gastronomic concept, designed for a more elevated dining experience.

For cocktails, KONG provides a vibrant setting throughout the day, while Cielo, its rooftop, offers stunning ocean views and a unique atmosphere, especially at sunset, with live music from Thursday to Saturday.

Papagayo Beach Club: day-to-night experience

Located in Playa de las Américas, Papagayo Beach Club offers a versatile experience that evolves throughout the day. Its gastronomy combines seafood, meats and international cuisine, alongside AWA restaurant, focused on Mediterranean flavours with Atlantic influence.

Its ocean views and sunset setting make it a perfect place to extend the day into the evening with music and live shows.

Las Rocas Beach Club: relaxation by the ocean

Las Rocas Beach Club is ideal for those looking for a more relaxed atmosphere. Natural pools, sunbeds and Balinese beds create a calm environment to enjoy the day.

Its Mediterranean and Canarian cuisine, combined with live music, completes a laid-back experience by the sea.

OA Beach Club: chill atmosphere by the Atlantic

OA Beach Club offers a more relaxed concept with lounge areas, restaurant and oceanfront spaces designed for slow-paced enjoyment.

Its cuisine focuses on fresh, local products with vegetarian options and sharing dishes, complemented by cocktails and a sunset-oriented atmosphere.
