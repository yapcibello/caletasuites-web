Autumn and winter make Costa Adeje one of the most attractive parts of Tenerife for travellers seeking peace, wellbeing and a mild climate. The area brings together sea, nature and premium services, making it ideal for slowing down and taking care of yourself. If you are staying at Caleta Suites, you’ll find some of the best spas in Costa Adeje just a few minutes away. Here are three exclusive options to restore energy, unwind and reconnect with yourself.

Bio-Spa Victoria – GF Victoria (Costa Adeje)

Among the most highly rated spas in Costa Adeje, Bio-Spa Victoria stands out for its holistic approach to wellbeing. Located inside the GF Victoria hotel, it has received international awards for excellence. Its thermal circuit includes a cervical jet pool, sauna, Turkish bath, jacuzzi, ice fountain and sensory showers. Outside, you’ll find Balinese beds, a garden area and a counter-current swimming pool.

One of its most popular plans is the “Circuit + Brunch”, perfect for enjoying a relaxing morning followed by a healthy, high-quality meal. It’s an ideal spa for those who value sustainability, calm and a serene environment.

Bahía Wellness Retreat – Bahía del Duque (Costa Adeje)

The Bahía Wellness Retreat is another of the most exclusive spas in Costa Adeje. Inspired by the volcanic nature of Tenerife, it offers a beautiful outdoor thalassotherapy circuit surrounded by gardens. Its seawater pools provide therapeutic benefits and a truly sensory experience.

The spa features personalised rituals, relaxing massages, premium facials and wellness programmes designed to reduce stress and balance the body. The atmosphere is elegant, quiet and meticulously cared for. It’s perfect for a day dedicated entirely to self-care in a luxurious setting.

Wellness Corales – Royal Hideaway Corales Resort (La Caleta, Costa Adeje)

In the heart of La Caleta, Wellness Corales offers an intimate and elegant space just a short walk from Caleta Suites. Located inside the Royal Hideaway Corales Resort, it is designed exclusively for adults and guarantees calm and privacy.

Its facilities include a hydromassage pool, sauna, steam room and dedicated relaxation areas. Treatments and massages use high-quality products and techniques inspired by the Indian Ayurvedic tradition. Access is available to external guests with prior reservation, making it an excellent option for enjoying one of the best spas in Costa Adeje without being a hotel guest.
