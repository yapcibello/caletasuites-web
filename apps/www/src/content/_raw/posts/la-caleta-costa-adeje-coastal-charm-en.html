Nestled in the heart of Costa Adeje, La Caleta is a true hidden gem along the Tenerife coastline — a place where the island’s fishing heritage meets a refined, laid-back lifestyle. This former fishing village has managed to preserve its authentic Canarian essence while becoming one of the most exclusive destinations in southern Tenerife.

Walking through La Caleta Costa Adeje means embracing a slower pace of life, guided by the rhythm of the sea. Its narrow streets lead to volcanic rock coves, its terraces overlook the Atlantic, and its relaxed atmosphere invites you to enjoy every moment without hurry. It’s the perfect spot for those who value tranquillity, comfort and natural beauty.

What to see and do in La Caleta Costa Adeje

While its main charm lies in its serenity, La Caleta Costa Adeje offers a wide variety of experiences that combine relaxation, nature and top-tier gastronomy.

Stroll along the Atlantic

The seaside promenade connecting La Caleta with the centre of Costa Adeje is one of the most pleasant walks in the south of Tenerife. Along the way, you’ll find viewpoints, hidden coves and terraces — ideal for a gentle walk, light exercise or simply enjoying the ocean views. As evening falls, the sun melts into the sea, painting some of the most spectacular sunsets on the island.

Taste the finest coastal gastronomy

Gastronomy is at the heart of La Caleta Costa Adeje’s identity. Here you’ll find some of the best restaurants in southern Tenerife — from traditional seafood taverns serving freshly caught fish, such as La Vieja, to Michelin-starred dining experiences like El Rincón de Juan Carlos (two stars) or San-Hô (one star). Dining by the sea becomes a ritual, where each dish celebrates local produce and the art of good living.

Enjoy sports in a privileged setting

The surroundings of La Caleta Costa Adeje are ideal for sports lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Just a few minutes away lies the prestigious Golf Costa Adeje, an international reference for golf aficionados, and Tenerife Top Training, a high-performance sports centre chosen by elite athletes from around the world. Thanks to Tenerife’s stable climate year-round, you can enjoy activities such as coastal hiking, paddle surfing or open-water swimming in any season.

An elegant and relaxed lifestyle

More than a holiday destination, La Caleta Costa Adeje embodies a way of life. Here, luxury isn’t about opulence — it’s about calm, authenticity and wellbeing.

Those who choose La Caleta Costa Adeje are looking for balance and serenity: breakfasts overlooking the ocean, leisurely afternoon walks, exceptional cuisine and the soothing rhythm of the waves. It’s a place where time slows down, leaving room for life’s small pleasures — the sea breeze, the light and the peace of Tenerife’s southern coast.
