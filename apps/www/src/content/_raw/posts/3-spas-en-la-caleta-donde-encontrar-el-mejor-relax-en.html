In a world where stress and anxiety are increasingly present in our daily lives, there is nothing better than slowing down and taking a breath. And for that, spas are the perfect place: a true oasis of tranquillity and self-care that we so often put off.

What better way to complete your holiday than with a massage, a thermal bath and a therapeutic treatment? We present three spa options near La Caleta where both body and mind can truly recharge.

Anantara Spa

At Anantara Spa, the best journeys are felt… not told. This is the philosophy of this space born in Thailand and now established in Adeje, where guests can experience ancient Eastern healing traditions. Here, the latest in wellness is combined with science, exploring relaxing rituals for body and mind, harmoniously blended with the rhythms of the island of Tenerife.

A highly acclaimed option among visitors, it is the perfect place to hide away, unwind and truly live in the present moment.

Wellness Corales

Wellness Corales offers a complete relaxation circuit featuring a hydrotherapy pool with a cold plunge pool, tubular benches, swan neck jets, a cobra cascade and a semi-outdoor pool.

To complement the hydrotherapy experience, the spa also includes a sauna and a steam room, which help eliminate toxins through perspiration and promote easier breathing.

This is an ideal choice for those looking to find a moment of deep relaxation while also taking care of their overall health and wellbeing.

Nammu Spa Areas 

Just a five-minute drive from La Caleta lies this haven of peace. Its innovative facilities take relaxation to the next level with comprehensive wellness programmes. Nammu Spa Areas features three personalised thermal circuits—Canarian, Celtic and Thalassa—as well as sixteen treatment rooms offering a wide range of facial and body treatments focused on health and beauty.

The perfect moment to take care of yourself starts here.
