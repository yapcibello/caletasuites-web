Beyond its renowned seafood restaurants and cosy local taverns, La Caleta also knows how to satisfy your sweet cravings. From artisanal bakeries tempting visitors with freshly baked cakes and pastries to modern cafés serving irresistible sweet creations, this article will guide you to the perfect spot for that after-meal moment when someone says, “I could really go for something sweet.”

La Gelatería


We start with a true classic: ice cream. La Gelatería is a delightful option to enjoy with family and friends. It offers artisanal ice creams made with natural ingredients and genuine Italian craftsmanship. In addition, you’ll find a wide variety of smoothies and different types of brioche to suit every taste.


Bloom


With its stylish décor, Bloom has already become a must-visit spot if you are spending a few days in La Caleta. Pancakes, waffles, crêpes and a tempting selection of Belgian sweets make up its menu.


Whether you’re in the mood for something fruit-based or prefer a more traditional chocolate treat, there is always something ready to satisfy your cravings.



Taste Crepería & Bistro


Located in Plaza del Duque Shopping Centre, very close to La Caleta, Taste Crepería & Bistro is considered by many of its customers to serve the best crêpes in Tenerife.


Inspired by a traditional grandmother’s recipe, their menu includes crêpes filled with Nutella, fresh fruit, berries or ice cream, among many other delicious options.



Cafetería Boulangerie 


Just a 10-minute drive from La Caleta, Cafetería Boulangerie is a colourful spot filled with high-quality ingredients. Alongside more than thirty types of sandwiches, it offers a wide range of smoothies, freshly baked pastries and a coffee so good it will make you want another… and another.


Cafetería Boulangerie also caters to vegan and gluten-free diets, so there’s no excuse not to stop by and enjoy something sweet in this inviting space.
