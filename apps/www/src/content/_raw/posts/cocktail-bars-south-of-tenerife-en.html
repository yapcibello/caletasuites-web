Saying goodbye to the year by the sea is one of the most enjoyable ways to experience the final days of December. The south of Tenerife, with its mild climate and relaxed atmosphere, offers the perfect setting to do so at an unhurried pace, cocktail in hand and the Atlantic Ocean as a backdrop. If you are staying at Caleta Suites, you will find some of the best cocktail bars in the south of Tenerife just moments away, ideal for toasting before the New Year arrives.

Away from the buzz of New Year’s Eve itself, the days leading up to it invite you to enjoy peaceful terraces, carefully crafted cocktails and spaces where time seems to slow down. From hotels close to La Caleta to characterful venues across the south of the island, these spots combine views, atmosphere and a refined cocktail offering.

Zambra Sky Bar: views and elegance just minutes from La Caleta

Located on the rooftop of the GF Victoria hotel, Zambra Sky Bar is one of the cocktail bars closest to Caleta Suites and an excellent choice for enjoying a sunset drink. Its rooftop offers open views over the ocean and an elegant setting, perfect for raising a glass during the final days of the year in a calm and relaxed atmosphere.

The cocktail menu focuses on balanced combinations and fresh flavours, designed to be enjoyed in a refined environment that lends itself perfectly to a slow and unhurried farewell to the year.

Maresía Atlantic Bar: cocktails by the ocean

Very close to La Caleta, within the Royal Hideaway Corales Beach, Maresía Atlantic Bar stands out for its privileged seafront location. This space combines a refined cocktail proposal with an elegant and tranquil setting, ideal for those looking to toast the end of the year in a more exclusive environment.

Its terrace invites guests to enjoy the sound of the ocean alongside a cocktail menu created to accompany the last sunsets of the year in the south of Tenerife.

La Vieja: a special mention in La Caleta

In the heart of La Caleta, La Vieja is widely known for its seafood-focused cuisine and its commitment to fresh fish and local shellfish throughout the year. Beyond its gastronomic offering, the restaurant also features a cocktail menu that encourages guests to linger after lunch or simply stop by for a drink by the sea.

Its terrace, set right next to the ocean, is a privileged spot for an afternoon toast, with uninterrupted Atlantic views and the peaceful atmosphere of this small fishing village. La Vieja also offers a special New Year’s Eve menu on 31 December, making it an ideal choice for those wishing to say goodbye to the year with a complete dining experience by the sea.
