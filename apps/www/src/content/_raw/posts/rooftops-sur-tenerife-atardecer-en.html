South Tenerife is known for its privileged climate, Atlantic views and a lifestyle that combines gastronomy, music and unique landscapes. Among the most popular plans for both travellers and residents are the rooftops in South Tenerife, elevated spaces where you can watch the sunset while enjoying a cocktail and a relaxed atmosphere.

From La Caleta, one of the most peaceful and exclusive areas of Costa Adeje, it is easy to reach some of the most interesting rooftops in the south of the island. These are four places where the sunset becomes an experience in itself.

Zambra Sky Bar – GF Victoria (Costa Adeje)

Among the rooftops in South Tenerife closest to La Caleta is Zambra Sky Bar, located at the GF Victoria hotel in Costa Adeje. This elevated venue offers open views of the Atlantic and a stylish setting where guests can enjoy cocktails while the sun sets over the ocean.

The concept blends contemporary design with a carefully curated drinks menu that complements the sunset experience. Its proximity to La Caleta and Caleta Suites makes it an especially convenient option for those who want to enjoy a rooftop without travelling far.

Maresía Atlantic Bar – Royal Hideaway Corales Resort (La Caleta)

Very close to Caleta Suites you will find Maresía Atlantic Bar, located at the Royal Hideaway Corales Resort, right in the heart of La Caleta. This rooftop is known for its refined cocktail concept and its privileged position overlooking the ocean, with views stretching towards the island of La Gomera.

Maresía has become one of the most appealing rooftops in South Tenerife for those seeking a relaxed and sophisticated setting near La Caleta. Its location allows guests to enjoy sunset drinks before returning comfortably to Caleta Suites.

Cielo – Monkey Beach Club (Costa Adeje)

Located within the iconic Monkey Beach Club in Costa Adeje, Cielo is one of the rooftops in South Tenerife that best combines atmosphere, music and sea views. This rooftop sits above the well-known beach club, creating a sophisticated setting perfect for enjoying the end of the day.

From its terrace you can admire some of the most spectacular sunsets along the coast while enjoying cocktails in a relaxed atmosphere. In addition, live music takes place from Thursday to Saturday, making each visit a lively and memorable experience.

For guests staying in La Caleta, it is a perfect evening plan in nearby Costa Adeje.

The 16th Sky Lounge – Hard Rock Hotel Tenerife (Playa Paraíso)

Another rooftop worth visiting in South Tenerife is The 16th Sky Lounge, located on the top floor of the Hard Rock Hotel Tenerife in Playa Paraíso.

This rooftop offers panoramic ocean views from a significant height, creating an impressive setting to watch the sunset. The experience combines music, cocktails and contemporary design, making it one of the most striking rooftop venues in the south of the island.

Sunset lifestyle near La Caleta

The growing presence of rooftops in South Tenerife reflects the evolution of Costa Adeje’s leisure scene, where gastronomy, design and landscape come together to create sophisticated experiences.
