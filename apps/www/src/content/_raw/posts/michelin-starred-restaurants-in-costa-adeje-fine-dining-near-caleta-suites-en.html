Fine dining in Costa Adeje

Among the Michelin-starred restaurants in Costa Adeje, these six stand out for their personality and international recognition:

San-Hô

A bold fusion of Japanese, Peruvian and Canarian flavours. San-Hô delivers a creative, precise and surprising culinary proposal, designed for those looking for a truly distinctive gastronomic experience.

El Rincón de Juan Carlos

A benchmark for haute cuisine in the Canary Islands. This restaurant offers an experience rooted in exceptional produce, technical mastery and emotion, constantly evolving while remaining deeply connected to its surroundings.

Il Bocconcino

Italian tradition interpreted through the lens of fine dining. Il Bocconcino stands out for its respect for classic recipes, high-quality ingredients and elegant execution, earning its place in the Michelin Guide.

 

Donaire


Contemporary cuisine with a strong focus on local ingredients and visual presentation. Donaire’s menus reflect seasonality and landscape, creating a refined and well-balanced dining experience.

Haydée by Víctor Suárez


An intimate and personal project that reinterprets Canarian cuisine from a modern perspective. Haydée is known for its technical precision, proximity to the product and a narrative deeply rooted in local identity.

NUB


Conceptual fine dining that connects territories and cultures through flavour. At NUB, creativity, storytelling and presentation come together to create a cohesive and immersive gastronomic experience.
