Sunsets — such a simple, everyday phenomenon, yet one that never fails to amaze. The blend of warm, reddish tones becomes a pause after the day’s hustle, a moment of calm and reflection, a scene to enjoy with your partner or the perfect backdrop for sharing time with friends. And all of these moments are even better when there’s something to toast with.

La Caleta is the ideal place to capture unforgettable sunsets while enjoying a well-crafted cocktail. That’s why we reveal the cocktail bars with the best views in this coastal enclave, where sight and taste come together in perfect harmony.

Maresía Atlantic Bar

Located at Royal Hideaway Corales Beach, Maresía is a rooftop bar offering some of the most spectacular panoramic sunset views, with La Gomera as a stunning backdrop.

The venue also features a signature cocktail menu where 100% of the ingredients used are of Canarian origin, making the most of seasonality and the freshness of local produce.

Altamira Chillout & Cocktail Bar

Nestled beside El Beril Beach, Altamira is a place where breathtaking sunsets meet a sophisticated and contemporary atmosphere.

This oasis of indulgence boasts a cocktail menu in constant evolution. From timeless classics to exclusive creations, each drink is carefully crafted to satisfy even the most discerning palates.

Restaurant 88

If innovation is your thing, this is the place to be. The vibrant colours of the Atlantic as it merges with the setting sun are reflected in the cocktails at Restaurant 88, renowned for reimagining traditional drinks.

A wasabi-infused margarita or a caipirinha with a spicy twist are just some of the options on a varied menu that blends local ingredients with subtle Japanese influences, all with one clear goal: to create a truly unique gastronomic experience.
