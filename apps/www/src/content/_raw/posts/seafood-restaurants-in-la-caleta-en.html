In the heart of Costa Adeje, La Caleta is one of those places where the ocean is part of everyday life. Its quiet streets, small harbour and terraces overlooking the Atlantic have made it an essential stop for those seeking great food and local charm. Here, the catch comes straight from the sea and is prepared with the authentic touch of traditional Canarian cuisine. So if you’re staying at Caleta Suites, don’t miss the chance to visit some of the best seafood restaurants in La Caleta.

La Vieja – a true classic in La Caleta

Among the many seafood restaurants in La Caleta, La Vieja is a true local institution. Open for more than two decades, this restaurant keeps the fishing spirit of the village alive. Its menu focuses on fresh fish and local shellfish, featuring signature dishes such as fried octopus, grilled parrotfish (vieja, a local favourite) or limpets with mojo sauce.

With views over the small harbour and a relaxed atmosphere, every meal here feels like a special experience — perfect for those who want to enjoy authentic flavours from the sea without losing the charm of the surroundings.

Salitre Restaurante – elegance and flavour by the Atlantic

Just a few steps from the water, Salitre Restaurante blends elegance, simplicity and excellent local produce. Its cuisine draws inspiration from the Atlantic and seasonal ingredients, with a variety of fresh fish, seafood and rice dishes that change throughout the year.

The restaurant is bright and contemporary, featuring a beautiful terrace where you can enjoy lunch or dinner with ocean views. It’s an ideal choice for visitors looking for seafood restaurants in La Caleta with a more modern touch, yet true to Canarian roots.

Restaurante La Caleta – tradition by the sea

Located right on the waterfront, Restaurante La Caleta is another must-visit spot for seafood lovers. Its menu combines traditional Canarian recipes with the flavours of the daily catch.

Here you can enjoy a simple grilled fish platter, sizzling prawns in garlic, or the typical vieja a la espalda (whole parrotfish grilled with olive oil and garlic). Friendly service and a terrace overlooking the Atlantic make this place the perfect choice to enjoy the sea, the sun and the most authentic Costa Adeje cuisine.
