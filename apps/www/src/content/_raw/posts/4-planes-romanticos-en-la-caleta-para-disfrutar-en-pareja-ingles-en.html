A moonlit evening shared over a delicious dinner, a sunset with views over the Atlantic Ocean, or a leisurely walk along the coast. La Caleta brings together all the perfect plans to enjoy as a couple. If you are looking for a place to celebrate an anniversary, a birthday, or simply enjoy a romantic moment together, here are four ideas to experience with your loved one in this charming coastal village in Tenerife.

Dinner by moonlight

Evenings in La Caleta are on another level. Its pleasant climate, combined with breathtaking views of the vast ocean and a gastronomy rich in local flavours, make it the perfect setting for a romantic dinner.

Whether you are in the mood for fresh fish, quality meat or Asian-inspired flavours, La Caleta offers plenty of options. Take a look at our article Our 6 favourite restaurants for dinner in La Caleta, where you will find a selection of the best restaurants in the area.

Catamaran cruise

If you would like to surprise your partner with an adventure, we have the perfect idea. You can enjoy a catamaran excursion along the beautiful coastline of Costa Adeje, taking in the highlights of this part of southern Tenerife while enjoying a romantic lunch on board.

Picnic on the beach

If you prefer a seaside lunch, La Caleta offers several beaches where you can enjoy a picnic on the sand, with the sound of the sea as the soundtrack to your date. From peaceful coves to more wild and unspoilt spots, this area caters to every taste.

In our post 4 beaches to discover La Caleta, you will find the best places to bring this plan to life.

Sunset cocktails

Who doesn’t enjoy watching the sunset with a drink in hand and good company — especially when that company is your partner? In La Caleta, the last light of the day blends with the deep blues of the sea, creating an idyllic scene that is a true pleasure to enjoy from a terrace. In our article The perfect pairing: cocktails and sunsets in La Caleta, you will find several spots where you can enjoy a great cocktail with even better views.
