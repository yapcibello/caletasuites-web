Arriving from ancient Japan, sushi has become a dish enjoyed all over the world, following a journey rich in history and flavour. Along the way, it has absorbed local influences, giving rise to new creations that fuse Japanese culture with the culinary traditions of different places — including the Canary Islands.

If you are a sushi lover, here are some of the best places in La Caleta to enjoy excellent sushi, from the most traditional expressions to the most innovative proposals.

Kai La Caleta

At Kai, the traditional Japanese izakaya concept is brought back to life, an aesthetic that is reflected not only in the space itself but also in its dishes. Their sushi stands out for blending flavours from different culinary cultures: classic Peruvian ingredients such as chilli, potato and coriander are combined with raw fish, seaweed and tempura, creating a truly distinctive dining experience.

Restaurant 88

The chefs at Restaurant 88 add a contemporary twist to classic Japanese sushi by incorporating subtle Canarian influences. Using fresh ingredients and refined culinary techniques, they create an experience that engages all the senses and offers a unique take on this iconic cuisine.

Deba Sushi

Just a ten-minute drive from La Caleta, Deba Sushi was born from the collaboration of two creative chefs determined to take sushi in a new direction.

Its name comes from the traditional Japanese knife, a key element in the preparation of their dishes. Here, sweet and savoury flavours come together to create sushi that is sharp and expressive, both in presentation and taste.

El Rincón del Sushi

Sushi, yes — but not just any sushi. That is the philosophy behind El Rincón del Sushi, a venue located very close to La Caleta where every bite becomes a unique experience.

Their creations cater to all tastes: whether you are a true sushi enthusiast or discovering Japanese cuisine for the first time, don’t miss the chance to enjoy their bold and flavourful combinations.
