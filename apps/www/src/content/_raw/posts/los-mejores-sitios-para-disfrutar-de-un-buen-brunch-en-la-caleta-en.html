Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, which is why it deserves to be enjoyed with the very best flavours. In recent years, brunch—that perfect blend of breakfast and lunch enjoyed around midday—has become a popular trend and has also made its way to La Caleta. For this reason, we present some of the best places in this coastal enclave to enjoy this culinary option, guaranteed to make early mornings feel far more appealing.

Matisse

A cosy spot where you can enjoy the finest artisanal delights: freshly baked croissants, sandwiches, toasties, and a wide selection of natural juices and smoothies with the sweetest combinations. Matisse defines itself as “a morning culinary hug”, offering fresh, high-quality products that will have your mouth watering just by reading the menu.

Bloom

At Bloom, they believe that a great day starts with an exceptional breakfast, which is why they have created a menu designed to satisfy even the most refined tastes. From poached eggs to pancakes, alongside a wide variety of drinks, there is something for everyone. For those with a sweet tooth, Bloom also offers an unlimited brunch, allowing you to enjoy coffee, wine, cava, beer or soft drinks as many times as you like over a two-hour period.

And don’t forget to try their house speciality: the Nutella Over Fruits pancakes.

La Cava

La Cava is the perfect choice for the indecisive, offering three different brunch options, each filled with a variety of delicious choices. From sandwiches made with the finest local ingredients to bagels, sweet or savoury fruit bowls… Whether you are in the mood for something light and healthy or a generous breakfast with no compromises, this is the place for you.
