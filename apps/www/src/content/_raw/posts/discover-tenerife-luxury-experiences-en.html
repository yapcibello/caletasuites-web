Welcome to our space for lovers of exclusive travel. Today we invite you on a journey through Tenerife, a destination where luxury, adventure and natural beauty come together. Here are seven of the best experiences to enjoy this gem of the Canary Islands.

1. Water Sports for Every Taste

Tenerife is a true paradise for water sports enthusiasts. From surfing, kitesurfing and wingfoiling at the iconic El Médano beach, to thrilling surf sessions in Las Américas, and freediving or scuba diving in the crystal-clear waters of Radazul or La Caleta, the island offers exciting options for all levels and preferences.

2. World-Class Cycling Routes

Cycling lovers will find some of the most spectacular routes winding through Teide National Park. Challenging climbs and breathtaking views make this a truly unique experience for those seeking adventure during their luxury holiday in Tenerife.

3. The Charm of Historic Villages

La Orotava and Garachico are perfect examples of Tenerife’s historic villages, where culture and tradition can be felt in every street and square. These picturesque towns are a must for travellers who enjoy urban and cultural tourism.

4. Exotic Beaches

El Bollullo Beach, in Puerto de la Cruz, and Las Vistas Beach, in Los Cristianos, are just two of the many extraordinary beaches Tenerife has to offer. Ideal for relaxing or enjoying time with family and friends in a truly special setting.

5. Hiking and Nature Exploration

The trails of Anaga Rural Park and the ascent to Mount Teide are unforgettable experiences for nature lovers. These routes offer stunning views and a deep connection with Tenerife’s unique landscapes.

6. Fun for the Whole Family

Siam Park and Loro Parque guarantee fun for visitors of all ages, making them perfect choices for those looking for luxury accommodation in Tenerife with family-friendly entertainment options.

7. Whale and Dolphin Watching

The coast of Los Gigantes is one of the best places to embark on a whale and dolphin watching excursion, a magical and relaxing experience that brings you closer to the marine life of the Atlantic.

Tenerife’s gastronomic scene is as diverse as it is refined, ranging from traditional local restaurants to Michelin-starred dining experiences, such as MB at The Ritz-Carlton, Abama.
