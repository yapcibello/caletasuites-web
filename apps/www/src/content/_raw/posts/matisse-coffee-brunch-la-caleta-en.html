Travelling is also about slowing down and enjoying time at your own pace. Starting the day with a good coffee, stretching the morning with a relaxed brunch or opting for a light lunch without leaving your surroundings can truly enhance your stay. In La Caleta, that sense of comfort comes naturally, and one of the best examples is Matisse Coffee & Brunch, a café located in the same building as Caleta Suites, designed to enjoy brunch in La Caleta at any time of day.

Open daily from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm, Matisse offers a versatile menu that adapts perfectly to different moments: leisurely breakfasts, unhurried brunches, something to snack on between meals or even a casual lunch, always with carefully selected ingredients and an elegant presentation.

Brunch in La Caleta with a varied and well-balanced menu

Matisse’s culinary proposal blends classic favourites with more contemporary options. Its menu features focaccias, sandwiches, toast, yoghurt bowls, pancakes and a complete selection of egg dishes, ranging from a traditional English breakfast to the signature Matisse eggs or low-temperature eggs with more refined accompaniments.

For those looking for something lighter, the café offers well-crafted salads such as burrata with tomato, chicken curry with yoghurt, salmon salad or tuna belly. These are complemented by poke-style bowls with tuna, salmon or tofu, ideal for a balanced lunch within the brunch in La Caleta concept.

The menu is rounded out with main dishes like burgers, salmon, couscous or vitello tonnato, as well as a selection of small plates to share: brioche with foie gras and jam, croquettes, mini toasts with Cantabrian anchovies, wholegrain bruschettas or cheese and cured meat boards. Sweet treats, homemade cakes and freshly prepared smoothies complete the experience at any time of day.

A comfortable, elegant Mediterranean-inspired space

Matisse’s interior design reflects its culinary identity. Inspired by Mediterranean style, the space combines white, green and blue tones that create a fresh and luminous atmosphere. The result is a modern, welcoming and relaxed setting, perfect for enjoying a quick coffee or lingering over a long brunch or lunch in refined surroundings.

This balance between design and comfort reinforces Matisse’s position as a go-to café for those seeking brunch in La Caleta with style and ease.

Caleta Suites comfort, also at the table

One of Matisse’s greatest advantages is its direct connection with Caleta Suites. Guests will find the café’s menu inside their apartments and can place orders to enjoy comfortably in their own space. Breakfast on a private terrace, a relaxed brunch or a light meal without leaving the complex is all part of an experience designed to make the most of every moment.
