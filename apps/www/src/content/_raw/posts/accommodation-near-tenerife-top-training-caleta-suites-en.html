Tenerife Top Training, located in Costa Adeje, has become one of Europe’s leading high-performance sports centres. Professional teams, swimmers, triathletes and athletes from different disciplines choose its facilities throughout the year thanks to the island’s stable climate, its wide range of training options and its state-of-the-art equipment.

Just a few minutes from the complex you’ll find Caleta Suites, a premium accommodation option that offers comfort, privacy and a strategic location for those visiting T3 for both training and wellbeing.

Tenerife Top Training: an international benchmark

Tenerife Top Training features Olympic-size swimming pools, football pitches, specialised strength and cardio areas, outdoor courts and facilities adapted to a wide range of sports. Its privileged climate allows athletes to train outdoors all year long —an essential advantage for those seeking continuity and optimal preparation conditions.

La Caleta: sport, sea and a relaxed way of life

La Caleta is an ideal coastal spot for those who want to combine training with quality of life. Its seaside paths, small coves and calm atmosphere create the perfect setting for stretching sessions, a gentle walk after training or simply unwinding before the next workout.

The area also offers excellent local gastronomy, with restaurants that focus on fresh, high-quality ingredients —perfect for athletes who want to eat well while taking care of themselves.
