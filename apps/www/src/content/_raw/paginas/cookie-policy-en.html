  1. What is a Cookie?

The website www.caletasuitestenerife.com uses cookies and similar technologies that store and retrieve information when you browse. These technologies serve various purposes, such as:

✔ Recognizing you as a user.
✔ Obtaining information about your browsing habits.
✔ Customizing the way content is displayed.

The specific types of cookies we use are described in the following sections.

For more details on all types of cookies, you can refer to the Cookie Guide published by the Spanish Data Protection Agency.

      3.What Types of Cookies Do We Use?

a) Based on Ownership

🔹 First-party cookies: These are sent to the user’s device from a system or domain managed by Caleta Suites to provide the service requested by the user.

b) Based on Purpose

🔹 Analytics Cookies: These allow the website owner to monitor and analyze user behavior. They are used to measure website activity, track visitor interactions, and improve services based on user data.

🔹 Preference or Personalization Cookies: These store user preferences to enhance their browsing experience. They can:
✔ Remember the preferred website language.
✔ Identify the user’s region for localized content.
✔ Store browser preferences for optimized access.
✔ Adjust text size, font, and page layout.

c) Based on Duration

🔹 Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies stored only while the user is browsing. They disappear once the session ends.

🔹 Persistent Cookies: These remain stored on the user’s device for a specified period, which can range from a few minutes to several years, as determined by the cookie provider.

  1. Intrusiveness Level of Cookies Used

Cookies are categorized based on their intrusiveness level, using a scale from 1 to 3.

🔹 Level 2: Includes anonymous cookies for internal website functionality, navigation, and content maintenance. This category also includes third-party cookies explicitly requested by users, such as social media plugins (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn) and embedded content (videos, maps).
