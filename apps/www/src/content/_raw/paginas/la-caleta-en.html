Discovering La Caleta: A Hidden Paradise in Tenerife

Photograph by Turismo de Tenerife

Welcome to La Caleta, a charming coastal corner located in the municipality of Adeje, in the south of the island of Tenerife.

A quiet village, ideal destination for those who want to enjoy the tranquility, the smell of the sea, the sound of the waves and unparalleled sunsets.

View Map

A Town with History

La Caleta has its roots in the port, fishing and agricultural activity and, although it is located very close to the busiest tourist areas of Tenerife, such as Costa Adeje, it maintains its essence, its tranquility, and is ideal for enjoying island life.

Much more than sun and beach

Photograph by Turismo de Tenerife

La Caleta offers a variety of unique experiences for guests who choose this beautiful village for their vacations. Beautiful walks to the beaches of Diego Hernandez or Playa de los Morteros, a small secluded beach.

For the most sporty people looking to stay active during their vacations, Tenerife Top Training is a high performance sports center with all the facilities.

What to do in La Caleta

Local Gastronomy

La Caleta is famous for its seafood restaurants, which are considered among the best on the island. Some of the essential places to dine are La Vieja, with a spectacular terrace with an incomparable sunset and where the product is the real protagonist, Char, where meat and embers predominate, or Rosso Sul Mare, for lovers of Italian cuisine. A stop at Rincón del Varadero, where you can enjoy local dishes, is also a must.
